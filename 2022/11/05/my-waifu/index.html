Nino Nakano

(中野 二乃, Nakano Nino) is one of the tsundere characters in Gotoubun no Hanayome, along with her sister Itsuki Nakano. She is the second sister of the Nakano quintuplets and her love interest is Fuutarou Uesugi. She acts like a mother towards the quintuplets, cooking for them and taking care of the household chores. Among the five sisters, she has the highest hostility towards Fuutarou Uesugi and often rebelled against him early in the series, to the point of initially using dangerous and even illegal methods to kick him out of the house.
